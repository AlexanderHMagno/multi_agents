Executive Summary

This 3-month, $5,000 campaign is designed to significantly elevate Cit-E Cycles' brand presence within British Columbia, positioning it as Canada's premier e-bike retailer. By strategically promoting the high-performance Moustache Dimanche 29 Gravel e-bikes, we aim to drive qualified foot traffic to Cit-E Cycles' showrooms, increase test rides, and reinforce the company's reputation as a trusted expert in e-bike sales, service, and support.

Value Proposition: Cit-E Cycles offers an unparalleled selection of over 20 e-bike brands and 300+ models, backed by over 100 years of combined retail experience. This campaign leverages the premium Moustache Dimanche 29 Gravel line—priced attractively at CA$5,899 and CA$7,499—to attract discerning outdoor enthusiasts and urban commuters in BC seeking advanced electric cycling solutions.

Key Objectives: Raise awareness, promote the Moustache Dimanche 29 Gravel line, drive foot traffic and test rides, and establish Cit-E Cycles as a trusted expert.

Campaign Strategy

Our strategy is built on a foundation of targeted digital marketing, compelling creative, and a focus on driving in-store experiences. We aim to connect with our audience by highlighting the joy, capability, and ease that the Moustache Dimanche 29 Gravel offers, while reinforcing Cit-E Cycles' position as the leading e-bike expert in British Columbia.

  • Strategic Pillars & Objectives: Position Cit-E Cycles as Canada's premier e-bike retailer, promote the Moustache Dimanche 29 Gravel line, drive foot traffic and test rides, and establish expertise in service and support.
  • Targeting Strategy: Hyper-local targeting (20-30km radius of BC showrooms) combined with interest-based targeting (cycling, outdoors, urban commuting, premium goods).
  • Messaging Strategy: Core themes include "Embrace the Adventure," "Effortless Exploration," "Expertise You Can Trust," and "Your BC Cycling Hub."
  • Positioning Strategy: Authoritative, trusted, and premium destination for e-bike solutions in BC.
  • Creative Development: Focus on stunning BC landscapes, dynamic riding shots, and the showroom experience.
  • Media & Channel Strategy: Prioritize paid social media (Facebook/Instagram), paid search (Google Ads), Local SEO, and micro-influencer collaborations within a $5,000 budget.
  • Measurement & KPIs: Track website traffic, social media engagement, test ride bookings, foot traffic, and lead generation.
  • Additional Requirements: Implement in-store test-ride events, utilize QR codes for booking, and highlight accessible service and financing options.
